Biryani Recipe

a set of instructions for preparing a particular dish, including a list of the ingredients required.

Ingredients

  • Put the boiling water in a saucepan, add the washed rice, boil for 5 minutes and drain.
  • Layer the chicken mixture with the rice starting with the rice then the chicken mixture and repeat this twice.
  • Lower the heat and simmer for a further 10 minutes

Method

  1. This recipe needs half kg chicken (1.1 lbs.). Make few slits on all the chicken pieces and add to a large bowl
  2. Mix everything well and marinate the chicken. Cover and set this aside for 1 hour.
  3. Meanwhile add 2 cups basmati rice to a large pot and rinse it at least thrice.
