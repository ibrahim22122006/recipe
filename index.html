    Scrambled Eggs
    Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.
    Igredients
      2 Eggs
      1tbs butter
      2tbs cream
    Method
    1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
    2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
    3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
    4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omlette)
    5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat 9th (will continue to cook on the omlatte until served)
